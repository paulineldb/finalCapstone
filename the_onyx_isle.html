World lore

The Onyx Isle can currently be found in a demiplane-like prison. It is ruled over by 6 gods (also known as Custodians) who are similar to well... prison wardens! They are not true gods but divine beings chosen by the Gods of their namesakes (Wealth, Death, Nature, Justice, Land and Knowledge).

The Isle was originally part of a larger world, from which it was broken from and trapped in a demiplane after a threat presented itself to the world and failed to be killed. There exist additional islands outside the purview of the "Gods".

Helia

Capital of the region: Viden

Helia is the twin brother to Hunraesa. He is studious and obsessed with the notion of advancing society, often to the detriment of his sister and himself as well.

When looking for students or prodigies, he is drawn to the ones that seek knowledge and self-betterment. Once a powerful Wizard, he chose to share what he knew (contrary to most of the other Wizards who hoard their knowledge), making him equally well-liked and hated amongst the magic community.

Many wizards who are brave enough to seek out magical knowledge have twisted his philosophy on sharing magic and knowledge to hoard it and keep it secret for themselves or use their knowledge to find other mages and report them to steal their secrets.

Depiction: Helia is depicted as a very old, serious Halfling man, most people's stereotypical vision of what a Wizard is (imagine a Hobbit-sized Gandalf).

Halivar

Capital of the region: Mereton

The region of Halivar is a monarchy, the throne of which is currently occupied by a king.

To this day, very littel is known about the custodian of the region. Halivar is known as the God of Physical Labor and is the prophet of the God of Balance.

Depiction: He is believed to take the form of a Leonin (a lion-like bipedal being).

Hunraesa

Capital of the region: Naefell

The region is a Democracy, lead by a Council found in the capital city.

Hunraesa is the twin sister to Helia, whose genius she worships. She loves him for his kind nature and gifting attitude.

She is a naturally gifted healer and excellent businesswoman who would fund her brother's research.

Her duty lays in maintaining health and keeping disease and pestilence at bay, which is why she is also seen as the Goddess of Disease and Decay. However, many still pray to her for deliverance from diseases or for protection from creatures that lurk in the darkness.

Depiction: She is depicted as a simple, kind, old woman. However, depictions of her as a hag can also be found.

Gytra

Capital of the region: Haagden

Gytra is a noble in the realm who was praised by the Lords and common folk alike for the reforms he proposed. He was chosen to judge those who died, working alongside Karzee. Due to his nature, he has a black and white sense of right and wrong which often causes disputes between himself and Karzee as she is far for forgiving.

He was allowed to keep his powers if he enforced the new laws set out by the new overlords. However, his duties changed from that of Law and Justice to Vengeance and Balancing.

He attempts to uphold the vow he made as close as possible within the confines of his new “prison”.

Depiction: A high elf noble with androgynous features, long blonde hair, athletic build, dressed in regal clothing fitting a deity of his station, a reminder that he was once one of the mortals.

Mortoth

Capital of the region: Torrenti

Mortoth is known for being extremely selfish and devoted to her family. She was the eldest child of two mothers, adopted at the age of 5, her younger siblings were other children her parents took in. She always felt that she owed her parents back and that she needed to prove herself. She became obsessed with bettering herself and her family’s station.

Mortoth was raised to protect families, to be a force in stopping evil entering their homes.

Many people prayed to her each night so that they would be protected from things that linger.

Many thieves’ guilds also have sects dedicated to her and their pursuit of wealth.

Depiction: Mortoth was described as a beautiful Gnome with long dark brown hair said to stop those who wish to do harm.
